The model

A simple equation for an invisible force.

Every pressure moment is the product of three forces: Importance, Uncertainty and Volume. Name a moment you are actually facing, then read it back. The equation shows you which force is driving, and where the lever is.

Once your people can read pressure, they can use the energy that comes with it instead of buckling under it. This is the same diagnosis we run in the room, in ninety seconds.

The book behind it

It started with one question.

"What's the most pressure you've ever been under?"

Dane Jensen asked that of thousands of people, from Olympic gold medalists to Navy SEALs to overworked parents, and found that pressure has patterns. The Power of Pressure, from HarperCollins, is the result.
