The idea that named us

You have more say in who you become than you think.

Studying people who reached remarkable levels of growth, Kazimierz Dąbrowski saw that the nature-versus-nurture debate was missing something: the role we play in our own development. He called it the third factor. Our whole purpose is to ignite it in others.

Est. 1991 · Toronto · Across ten Olympic Games
Founder · Dr. Peter Jensen

The man who made elite sport his laboratory.

Peter earned his PhD in sport psychology and studied under Dąbrowski himself. For decades he was a mental-training consultant to the Canadian Olympic team across roughly ten Games, including four consecutive medal-winning Women's hockey teams. He has taught a generation of MBAs as a top-rated instructor at Queen's Smith School of Business.

In 1991 he founded the firm, and wrote the books that became its foundation.

The Inside Edge Ignite the Third Factor Thriving in a 24/7 World
CEO · Dane Jensen

The son who took it into the age of AI.

Dane grew up where the dinner-table conversation was about performance under pressure. He studied at Queen's Smith, began in strategy consulting, then realized a strategy only survives if people can execute it when the pressure is on. So he came to the people side of the business.

As CEO he wrote The Power of Pressure, contributes regularly to Harvard Business Review, teaches at Queen's Smith and at UNC Kenan-Flagler, and sits on the board of the Canadian Paralympic Committee.

How we work

We live what we teach.
