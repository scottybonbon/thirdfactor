Working With People You Don't Understand

Work better with the people you don't get.

High-performing environments are made of strong individual contributors, and the strongest get results in a way that builds engagement in others. This is a practical guide to getting more of the results you want, and fewer of the ones you don't, while managing relationships and your own stress with the people you do not naturally gel with.

The model

Mindsets shape behaviors. Behaviors shape results.

The way we think about a situation governs how we act in it, and how we act decides what we get. Shift the mindset, and the rest follows.

Mindsets
The way we think

Adopt curiosity and generosity toward people who work differently.

Behaviors
The way we act

Those mindsets translate into productive, engaging behavior.

Results
What we achieve

Tangible results, and better relationships and experiences.

The two mindset shifts that do the work: curiosity and generosity.
Outcomes

What your people walk out able to do.

Get more of the results they want, with less of the stress.

→Understand why diversity of thought is a critical input to high-performing teams.
→See how their own mindsets contribute to the outcomes they do not want.
→Use curiosity and generosity to engage with people they do not gel with.
→Improve both their results and their experience of the work.
